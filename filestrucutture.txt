Step 4: Supabase Google OAuth Configuration

Enable Google OAuth:

In Supabase Dashboard > Authentication > Providers, enable Google.
Get your Google Client ID and Secret from the Google Cloud Console (APIs & Services > Credentials > Create OAuth Client ID).
Set the redirect URI to io.supabase.flutterquickstart://login-callback/.
